Instructions

  • The internship can be done at most once (if the insurance is provided by the university) during the summer term after taking courses from the 2nd year or the 3rd year.ARTICLE 6.3
  • An Occupational Health and Safety certificate must be obtained before the internship.ARTICLE 6.2
  • The internship is at least 20 working days and at most 48 working days.ARTICLE 7.1
  • The application form is filled out with the approval of the company and submitted to the department secretariat. The student finds the company where the internship will be done.ARTICLE 11
  • Applications are evaluated by the department internship committee.ARTICLE 5
  • The evaluation of the completed internship is done by the supervising faculty member.ARTICLE 10
  • The internship course (MIS 421) is selected in the last spring semester (in the eighth semester for regular students) and added to the transcript.ARTICLE 6.1
  • Since the internship course has elective course status (6 ECTS), it can be counted in place of another elective course after successful completion.
