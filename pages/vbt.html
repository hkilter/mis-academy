The information below is a summary of the Vocational Education in Business (VBT) process. For the original documents and forms in effect, please see the following links:
  • Faculty of Economics and Administrative Sciences, Vocational Education in Business Course Procedures and Principles
  • Forms

The purpose of VBT isARTICLE 1;

  • To provide students with the opportunity to reinforce their knowledge and experience,
  • To introduce workplaces and organizational structures,
  • To provide work discipline,
  • To provide the ability to use the information they have acquired in the department in practice,
  • To increase their employability,
  • To provide the habit of working together in team spirit.

Instructions

  • VBT is held for 14 weeks in the last spring semester (in the eighth semester for regular students).ARTICLE 5
  • 80% attendance is mandatory for VBT.ARTICLE 6
  • VBT is held in businesses that meet the criteria determined and announced by the department committee.ARTICLE 7
  • Students who register for VBT cannot register for another course that requires attendance.ARTICLE 8
  • It is the student's responsibility to find the business where VBT will be held.ARTICLE 10
  • An interim report must be submitted during the semester and a final report must be submitted at the end of the semester.
